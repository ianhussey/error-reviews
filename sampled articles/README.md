The database was compiled in the following manner (see the documented data processing code [here](https://github.com/ianhussey/error-reviews/tree/main/sampled articles/code) for more details): 

After exporting articles published in all psychology journals from CrossRef (in addition to a few hand-selected journals), articles were extracted from those journals that met the following eligibility criteria:

- Published between 2015 and 2025
- Accrued an average of 30 citations or more per year between 2015 and 2024
- The articles report empirical studies, methodological work, or meta-analyses
- No reviews or opinion articles without meta-analysis
- This resulted in 2092 articles.
